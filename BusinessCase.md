## The problem

School field trips, sports travel, and youth group outings rely on manual headcounts, group texts, and spreadsheets to track 20–150 students across unfamiliar environments. When a student is missing, chaperones have no systematic way to identify where that student was last seen, who verified their presence, or when the gap opened. This is a liability, a compliance risk, and a genuine safety failure waiting to happen.

---

## The solution

SafeRoster is a mobile-first group safety platform for K–12 school trips. Participants submit scheduled group selfies at defined intervals. The app uses AI facial recognition to automatically verify who is present, attaches GPS coordinates and a timestamp to each check-in, and surfaces a real-time roster to chaperones. Missed check-ins trigger escalating alerts before a window closes — not after.

- Photo-based check-in with AI face verification (on-device, privacy-first)
- Live chaperone dashboard: who checked in, who hasn't, last known location
- Critical-priority notifications that bypass Do Not Disturb
- COPPA/FERPA-compliant consent workflow with post-trip data purge

## Revenue model — freemium with paid safety features

| Tier | What's included | Price | Conversion lever |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | Manual roster, itinerary, group chat. Up to 15 travelers. | $0 | Build habit; hit the 15-traveler wall on first real trip. |
| **Safety (per trip)** | AI check-in, GPS tracking, critical alerts, up to 60 travelers. | **$49 / trip** | One incident or near-miss converts the chaperone permanently. |
| **District annual** | Unlimited trips, all schools in district, admin dashboard, SSO. | $2,400 / yr | Triggered after 3rd per-trip purchase at same school. |
